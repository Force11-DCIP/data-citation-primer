A Data Citation Primer

This primer is one of the outcomes of the Force11 Data Citation Implementation Pilot. It provides a quick overview over the main recommendations by the various working groups. It is directed at anyone interested in implementing data citation into the scholarly workflow. accesskey=" The site is split up by topics according to three key stakeholder groups: Authors, Journal Publishers and Editors, and Data Repositories
